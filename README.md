# Phone2.xyz

Phone2.xyz is the AI safety net for HVAC, plumbing, and electrical crews who live on Thumbtack/Angi leads. This single-page experience showcases the manager control room, contractor portal, onboarding steps, Safety Net and Lead Dialer modules, integration console, and ROI-driven messaging.

## Features
- Hero storytelling focused on "never buy the same lead twice" and "work on the roof, let us handle the phone."
- Manager login panel with live call log samples and status copy.
- Contractor portal access with enable/disable toggle for the AI assistant and status updates.
- Feature blocks for the Safety Net (missed-call text-back) and Lead Dialer (speed-to-lead) workflows.
- Onboarding flow + integration controls for numbers, forwarding, and voice selection.
- Interactive ROI calculator to quantify revenue preserved by Phone2.xyz.
